# Paper 1 Outputs

Final and diagnostic outputs for the command-area growth paper.

## Subdirectories

- `figures/`: manuscript-ready figures.
- `tables/`: manuscript-ready tables.
- `diagnostics/`: checks, alignment plots, and exploratory figures that support methods but are not final results.

Final figures and tables should be generated from CSVs in `Data/Processed/Paper1_CommandAreaGrowth/final_tables/`, not directly from hidden notebook state.
